About SparkleClean

We started SparkleClean because we saw neighbors and friends juggling jobs, families, and side projects — with no energy left to scrub sinks or mop floors. Our goal is simple: reliable cleaning you can book without stress.

Our mission

To give busy people their time back by delivering consistent, respectful cleaning that fits real schedules and real budgets.

Values we stand by

  • Reliability — we show up when we say we will.
  • Respect — your home and belongings are treated carefully.
  • Transparency — clear scope and pricing before we start.
  • Safety — vetted team members and sensible products.

Why trust us?

We use checklists, quality spot checks, and direct feedback loops so service improves over time. If something is missed, we work with you to make it right — because trust is built one visit at a time.
